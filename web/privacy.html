MYSTIC TAROT

Privacy Policy

Your inner world should remain yours. Mystic Tarot is designed around local-first storage and data minimization.

Information stored on your device

Mystic Tarot stores your chosen name, intention, reading journal, card collection, streak, XP, rituals, and preferences locally on your device. This information is used to personalize the experience and is not transmitted to us by the current release.

Information we collect

The current release does not require an account, does not include advertising SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not sell personal information. Support requests are optional and only contain information you choose to provide through GitHub.

Payments

The public web release does not process payments. If native subscriptions are introduced, payment information will be processed by Apple or Google; Mystic Tarot will not receive full card details. This policy and the store privacy disclosures will be updated before paid processing is activated.

Retention and deletion

Local records remain until you delete them from “Privacy & data,” clear browser storage, or uninstall the app. The in-app deletion control removes the journal, profile, collection, progress, and settings stored by Mystic Tarot on that device.

Children

Mystic Tarot is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Wellbeing and high-stakes decisions

Readings are symbolic prompts for reflection and entertainment. They are not medical, mental-health, legal, financial, or emergency advice.

Changes

Material changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date before they take effect.

Contact

For privacy or deletion questions, use the support page.
