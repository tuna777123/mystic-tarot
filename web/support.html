Support

Help with readings, your journal, privacy, installation, or access.

Quick fixes

  • Refresh the page if the app remains on the loading screen.
  • Use a current version of Safari, Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  • Do not clear site data if you want to keep your local journal.
  • Export your journal from Profile → Privacy & data before changing devices.

Install on your phone

On iPhone, open Mystic Tarot in Safari, choose Share, then “Add to Home Screen.” On supported Android browsers, use the Install prompt or browser menu.

Report a problem

Open a support request in the public Mystic Tarot issue tracker. Do not include private journal text, payment information, passwords, or other sensitive data.

Delete local data

Open Profile → Privacy & data → Delete all Mystic data. This permanently removes Mystic Tarot data stored on that device.

Emergency support

Mystic Tarot is not a crisis or emergency service. If you or someone else may be in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.
